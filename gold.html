The Gold Shop.

Levani Inc. first ever Shop. The Gold Shop has a unique design. It features a minecraft like chest(double chest). Filled with treasures. The Inspiration for this came from a german minecraft player.

On the Inside its made almost fully out of Wood. Its decked out with a Ender Chest and a Crafting Table. Being he first shop to have a Ender Chest and setting the trend. It has 2 Tables with Cashiers and a explanation of the product. In the middle above the Ender Chest are your most desired Items, Gold and XP Passes.


About the Goods

The Gold is produced at this Facility south from Spawn. The Facility was developed by Navynexus. It features a max-size Portal made out of high class Obsidian from the End. This Facility produces around 1500 Gold Ingots per hour. Players will be able to use this facility to get XP. Next to the Facility is a Enchanter, with wich you will be able to enchant all your items in no time.


XP PASS Instructions


  1. To arrive at the Facility use the Nether tunnel wich goes to the Stronghold. That Portal is clearly labeled. Follow the tunnel until you see a side tunnel to the right labeled as a Industrial Area, follow that tunnel to the end and enter the Portal. Here you will see the Facility. Climb up the Scaffolding and you arrived at the Facility.
  2. Now that you arrived open the door and enter the killing chamber pick up the trident and throw it on the ground. Like in the picture below.
  3. After you have thrown the trident and closed the door. There are 2 levers, activate both.
  4. After the Farm is activated. Stand on the AFK spot.
  5. Enjoy.

Useful Stuff

  • This Pass only includes XP, no Gold. If you want Gold buy it from the Shop.
  • Don't leave the Facility without turning it off.
  • Going near the door while it's active is dangerous.
  • Don't forget your Lapis Lazuli.
